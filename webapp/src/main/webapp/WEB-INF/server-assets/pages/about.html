About BioStudies

What is BioStudies?

The BioStudies database holds descriptions of biological studies, links to data from these studies in other databases at EMBL-EBI or outside, as well as data that do not fit in the structured archives at EMBL-EBI. The database can accept a wide range of types of studies described via a simple format. It also enables manuscript authors to submit supplementary information and link to it from the publication.

Publications / how to cite

  • Citing BioStudies database: please use the following publication: McEntyre J. et al., 2015. The BioStudies database.
  • Citing BioStudies submission in your manuscript: please include your experiment accession number and the URL to BioStudies home page, http://www.ebi.ac.uk/biostudies. e.g. "Data are available in the BioStudies database (http://www.ebi.ac.uk/biostudies) under accession number S-EPMC5090800."
